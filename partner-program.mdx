## Overview

The Partner Program is a way to earn extra income by promoting Motion Rental scooters. Whether you are a happy customer, a delivery rider, or know someone who could benefit, referring them earns real rewards.

## Partner success tips

- Share Authentically: Talk about your own experiences.
- Use Social Media: Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, YouTube.
- Target the Right Audience: Students, delivery riders, and commuters benefit most.
- Use Promotions: Take advantage of partner bonuses or seasonal campaigns.

## Benefits

- Passive Income: Earn for every successful referral.
- Help Others Save: Referrals gain access to affordable scooters.
- Free to Join: No costs, fees, or commitments.
- Partner Bonuses: Earn more with special promotions.
- Partner Tools: Access assets and resources to promote our service.

## Payouts

- Structure: Earn commission when a referral signs up for a subscription and stays subscribed for at least 4 weeks.
- Frequency: Payouts are made monthly, on the 7th. You must reach a minimum balance of $50 for a payout; any remaining balance rolls over to the next month.
- Method: Wise Transfer recommended for fast, simple payments.

<Callout kind="info">

**Partner payment note**

- Minimum payout: $50
- Commission Period: Once (active reward per referral)
- Commission Review: 35 days (referred subscriber counts after this period)
- Commission Claim: 3 years (unclaimed rewards expire)
- Only paid via Wise Mass Pay (ensure you have an AUD account)

</Callout>

## FAQs

<ExpandableGroup>

<Expandable title="What referral methods are allowed?">

Websites, blogs, YouTube, social media, company recommendations. Paid traffic or voucher-only networks are not accepted.

</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Why partner with Motion Rental?">

Help grow our subscription service and earn for your referrals. Gain access to dashboards, assets, and promotional tools.

</Expandable>

<Expandable title="How much commission do I get?">

$50 for every qualifying subscription (minimum 4-week rental).

</Expandable>

<Expandable title="What is a qualifying rental?">

A subscriber who rents a vehicle for at least 4 weeks.

</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Are there limits on referrals?">

No limit, but minimum $50 threshold must be reached for payout; excess rolls over.

</Expandable>

<Expandable title="How can I earn more?">

Use our assets on social media, forward content to groups, and share your referral link widely.

</Expandable>

<Expandable title="How do commission payouts work?">

Commissions are approved by the 1st of each month and paid on the 7th via Wise account.

</Expandable>

</ExpandableGroup>
