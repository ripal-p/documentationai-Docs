## Overview

Access key rental documents from Motion Rental when delivery platforms, official checks, or your own records require proof of insurance, registration, or your rental agreement.

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="TL;DR" default-open="true">
    - Motion Rental can provide your Insurance Certificate, Registration Certificate, and Rental Contract while your rental is active.

    - Submit all document requests through the Document Request Form on our website.

    - Use these documents for food delivery platforms, official checks, or your personal records.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>

## Documents we provide

We can supply specific documents for your scooter rental while your agreement is active and in good standing.

### Certificate of Currency (Insurance Certificate)

This confirms your scooter is covered under valid insurance.

You may need it for:

- Food delivery platform verification

- Proof of insurance for official checks

- Your own insurance or record-keeping

### Vehicle Registration Certificate

This is issued by the Department of Transport and Main Roads and confirms your scooter registration.

You may need it for:

- Food delivery platform verification

- Proof of current registration

- Official checks on the vehicle

### Rental Contract

This is the agreement that outlines your rental terms and conditions with Motion Rental.

You may need it for:

- Platform account verification

- Clarifying responsibilities and inclusions

- Your personal records or financial records

## Need other documents?

If you need a document that is not listed here, Motion Rental may still be able to help depending on what you need and why you need it.

Submit your request through the Document Request Form and include clear details about:

- Which document you are asking for

- Why you need it

- Any specific information or format required by a platform or agency

## How to request a document

Submit all document requests through the Document Request Form on our website so our team can assess and respond.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Document Request Form" title-type="p">
    Go to the Motion Rental website and open the Document Request Form or use the Important links below and navigate to Document Request Form.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your details" title-type="p">
    Add your contact details and rental information so we can confirm your identity and match the request to your scooter rental.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Specify the documents you need" title-type="p">
    Clearly list which documents you need, such as Certificate of Currency, Vehicle Registration Certificate, or Rental Contract, and why you need them.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit the form" title-type="p">
    Send the form. Our team will review your request and respond with the documents or next steps.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="danger" collapsed="false">
  **Important**

  Motion Rental may deny document requests if we suspect copying, forgery, duplication, or misuse of any documents.
</Callout>

## Final notes

Having the correct documents ready helps you avoid delays with delivery platform onboarding, official checks, and your own record-keeping.

If you are unsure which document you need, contact Motion Rental support or submit a Document Request Form with as much detail as possible.
