## Overview

Pay your Security Deposit, First Week’s Subscription Fee, and any approved manual charges using PayID instead of BSB and account numbers. Transfers are usually fast, secure, and straightforward with major Australian banks.

You can use PayID for:

- Security Deposit

- First Week’s Subscription Fee

- Approved manual payments

Ongoing weekly subscription fees are still processed automatically via direct debit and are not paid via PayID.

## What is PayID?

PayID lets you send money to a mobile number, email address, or ABN instead of entering a BSB and account number. Your banking app shows the business name linked to the PayID so you can confirm you are paying the correct business before you send money.

Motion Rental uses our ABN as our PayID, so you can pay us by selecting Pay to PayID in your bank and entering the ABN.

## Our PayID details

Use these details whenever you pay Motion Rental via PayID:

- **PayID type:** ABN

- **ABN:** `27 666 214 384`

- **Business name:** `Motion Rental Pty Ltd`

<Callout kind="alert" collapsed="false">
  Always confirm the business name shows exactly as `Motion Rental Pty Ltd` before you pay. If the name does not match exactly, stop and contact us immediately.
</Callout>

## Why use PayID?

PayID offers several benefits compared to paying by BSB and account number:

- **Fast:** Transfers usually arrive almost instantly, including on weekends and public holidays.

- **Secure:** Your bank shows the registered business name linked to the PayID before you confirm the payment.

- **Simple:** You do not need to enter BSB and account numbers, only the ABN PayID.

Select your bank below and follow the steps for either Internet banking or your mobile app. In all cases, use our ABN PayID and confirm the business name is `Motion Rental Pty Ltd`.
