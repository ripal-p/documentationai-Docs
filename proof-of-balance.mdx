## Overview

A Proof of Balance document confirms that:

- The bank account belongs to you
- Your name matches your subscription
- Your account is active

In most cases, it also shows your residential address.

One document can meet multiple verification requirements, which speeds up approval.

## Why we ask for a Proof of Balance document

### Proof of address verification

Proof of Balance documents usually display:

- Your full name
- Your residential address
- Your bank details

This allows us to confirm your contact details match your subscription.

### Proof of bank account ownership

All subscription payments are processed via direct debit.

We must confirm the bank account:

- Is registered under your name
- Matches the account used for your subscription

### Simplified verification

A Proof of Balance document can cover:

- Proof of address
- Proof of bank account ownership

Fewer documents means faster verification.

## How to download Proof of Balance from major Australian banks

<Callout kind="tip">

**Important**

Screenshots are not accepted. You must download the official PDF document from your bank.

</Callout>

<Tabs>

<Tab title="Commonwealth">

#### Internet banking

<Steps>

<Step title="Log in to NetBank">

Log in to your NetBank account with your customer number and password.

</Step>

<Step title="Open account information">

Go to **View accounts** and select **Account information**.

</Step>

<Step title="Select the correct account">

Choose the bank account you use for your subscription payments.

</Step>

<Step title="Open Balance confirmation">

Select **Balance confirmation** for that account.

</Step>

<Step title="Save as PDF">

Save or download the Balance confirmation as a PDF file to your device.

</Step>

</Steps>

#### Mobile app

<Steps>

<Step title="Log in to the CommBank app">

Open the CommBank app and log in.

</Step>

<Step title="Open your account">

Tap **Accounts** and select the account you use for your subscription.

</Step>

<Step title="Go to Proof of balance">

Tap **Manage**, then **Statements and more**, then **Proof of balance**.

</Step>

<Step title="Save as PDF">

Tap the **Share** icon and save the document as a PDF.

</Step>

</Steps>

</Tab>

<Tab title="NAB">

#### Internet banking

<Steps>

<Step title="Log in to NAB internet banking">

Sign in to NAB internet banking.

</Step>

<Step title="Find Proof of Balance">

Locate **Proof of Balance** in the side panel.

</Step>

<Step title="Download as PDF">

Select it for the correct account, then download and save the document as a PDF.

</Step>

</Steps>

#### Mobile app

<Steps>

<Step title="Open the NAB app">

Open the NAB app on your device.

</Step>

<Step title="Go to Reports">

Tap **More**, then **Reports**, then **Proof of balance**.

</Step>

<Step title="Save the PDF">

Select the correct account and save the generated PDF to your device.

</Step>

</Steps>

</Tab>

## Need help?

If you cannot find the Proof of Balance option in your banking app, contact your bank directly.

If you are unsure whether your document is acceptable, contact our support team before uploading.

## Final notes

Download the official PDF version only.

Providing the correct document from the start helps us verify your details faster and set up your subscription without delays.
