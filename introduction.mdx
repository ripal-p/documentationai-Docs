## TL;DR

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  Use Motion Rental Docs as your first stop for answers, the Forms page for actions and requests, and contact support only when you cannot solve it yourself.
</Callout>

## What these Docs are for

Motion Rental Docs give you one place to understand how your rental, payments, insurance, servicing, and support work.

Use them to:

- Learn what to expect before and during your rental.

- Check what information you need before submitting a request.

- Avoid back-and-forth with support by getting things right the first time.

## Why use self-service

Self-service helps you stay on the road with fewer delays.

Benefits include:

- **Faster support** — Solve common issues without waiting for a reply.

- **More control** — Handle bookings, reports, and requests on your schedule.

- **Fewer mistakes** — Know exactly what details you need to provide.

- **Clear guidance** — Follow step-by-step instructions for each task.

## How to use our Docs and forms

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check the Docs first" icon="book-open" title-type="p">
    Search or browse the Docs for topics like rental terms, insurance, payments, vehicle maintenance, and service or support. Most common questions and scenarios are answered here.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Use the Forms page for actions" icon="list-ordered" title-type="p">
    When you need to do something, go to the Forms page. Use it to book a scooter, report an incident, request documents, or submit a service or support request with all required details.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Contact the team only if needed" icon="help-circle" title-type="p">
    If the Docs and Forms do not cover your situation, reach out using the contact form. Our team then focuses on issues that genuinely need a person to step in.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What makes this system better

This self-service flow is designed to keep you riding, not waiting.

- **Faster turnaround** — Requests reach the right team with the right info.

- **More clarity** — You see what is required at each step before you submit.

- **Self-service convenience** — Manage everything from your phone, anytime.

- **Smarter support** — The team spends time on complex issues, not chasing missing details.

## Final notes

Make these Docs and forms your first stop whenever you have a question or need to take action, not your last resort.

Bookmark this page so you can quickly:

- Look up how something works.

- Confirm what information you need.

- Jump straight into the correct form.
