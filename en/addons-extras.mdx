## Overview

When renting from Motion Rental, you can choose optional extras to suit your needs and budget.

These extras provide:

- Added safety

- More flexibility

- Lower financial risk in case of an incident

You pay for each extra weekly, on top of your standard rental charges, and see them listed as separate lines on your invoice. All extras must be selected at sign-up or before your rental begins.

## Optional safety and convenience extras (weekly charges)

These extras help with day-to-day riding and security. The following extras are charged weekly:

### Security Lock — $1 per week

Reduces the risk of theft when parking your scooter.

### Extra Helmet — $5 per week

Suitable if you ride with a passenger or need a spare helmet.

## Insurance excess reduction options

All rentals include a standard insurance excess of $1,500.

You can reduce this excess by paying an additional weekly fee.

Available options:

- Reduce excess from $1,500 to $1,250 — $5 per week

- Reduce excess from $1,500 to $1,000 — $10 per week

- Reduce excess from $1,500 to $750 — $15 per week

- Reduce excess from $1,500 to $500 — $25 per week

Reducing your excess lowers the amount you may need to pay if an incident occurs, subject to your insurance terms.

## Important things to know

These points apply to all addons and extras, including safety gear and insurance options.

- All extras are optional.

- Request extras during sign-up or before your rental starts.

- The team may not always ask about extras, so tell the team if you want to add any.

- Extras appear on your invoice only.

- Extras do not appear on your rental contract.

- Your contract may still show the standard $1,500 excess. If you selected Insurance Excess Reduction, the reduced excess applies in the event of an incident.

## Final notes

Optional extras allow you to customise your rental.

If you are unsure which option suits you, speak with our team before your rental begins.

## FAQs

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="Do extras apply for the full rental period?" default-open="true">
    Yes. Extras apply for the entire duration of your rental unless we agree otherwise in writing.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Are extras charged weekly?" default-open="false">
    Yes. All extras are charged weekly and included in your regular invoice.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Why doesn’t my contract show the reduced insurance excess?" default-open="false">
    Your rental contract always shows the standard $1,500 excess.

    If you selected Insurance Excess Reduction, your invoice confirms the reduced excess you are paying for. That reduced excess applies if an incident occurs.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Can I add extras after my rental has started?" default-open="false">
    In most cases, extras must be selected before your rental begins.

    Contact our team to check whether changes can be made after your start date.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Are extras mandatory?" default-open="false">
    No. All extras are completely optional.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>
