## Overview

All Motion Rental vehicles include Comprehensive Insurance as part of your rental. This means you are covered while using the vehicle, including for food delivery services.

We keep our insurance simple and transparent so you know exactly where you stand.

## What is covered

Our Comprehensive Insurance covers:

- Accidental damage

- Third-party property damage

- Theft

- Total loss (subject to policy terms)

This cover applies whether you are using the scooter for personal transport or approved food delivery services.

## Insurance excess

All rentals include a standard insurance excess of $1,500.

The excess is the maximum amount you would be required to pay if there is an accident, theft, or total loss of the vehicle (subject to the rental agreement and policy terms).

## Optional insurance excess reduction

If you prefer lower financial risk, you can choose our optional Insurance Excess Reduction.

This reduces the amount you would need to pay in the event of an approved claim.

The reduced excess amount and weekly fee will be clearly shown on your invoice.

Please refer to the Addons & Extras Doc in the Related Docs section below for full details.

## Important notes

- The rental agreement always displays the standard $1,500 excess.

- If you select Excess Reduction, your invoice confirms the reduced amount that applies to you.

- All claims remain subject to the terms and conditions of the rental agreement.

## Final notes

We designed our insurance structure to be straightforward and easy to understand. If you are unsure about anything, contact our team before starting your rental so we can explain your options clearly.

## FAQs

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="Do I have to arrange my own insurance?" default-open="false">
    No. Comprehensive Insurance is included in all rentals. You do not need to arrange your own cover.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="What if I’m using the scooter for food delivery?" default-open="false">
    Our Comprehensive Insurance covers food delivery use. Some rental companies exclude this, so it is always important to check.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="How much is the insurance excess?" default-open="false">
